RED ONION

India is the second largest onion growing country in the world. Indian onions are famous for their pungency and are available round the year. Indian onions has two crop cycles, first harvesting starts in November to January and the second harvesting from January to May. We are topmost Red Onion (Nasik onion) Exporters from India.

We export onions in a variety of sizes. We also Exporters Fresh White Onion, Red Onion and Pink Onion from India. Onions belong to the allium family and are known globally for their pungent character and medical benefits. These onions are low in saturated fat, cholesterol and sodium. It is known to lower the risk of heart disease. Regular consumption is known to aid a prolonged life. Onions are low calorie foods, rich in various vitamins and minerals. These are used extensively in Indian cooking and is also sometimes consumed raw.
